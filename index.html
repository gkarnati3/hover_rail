Product Overview

A one stop shop to reducing net emissions from transportation vehicles on your campus.

Our novel technology is a radically modified rail system which allows people to be transported across campus through magnetic levitation.

This infrastructure is successful due to its various technologically advanced components which include Humalev, an artificially engineered pharmaceutical element, powerful magnets aka the S.L.I.M magnets, and the robust rail system itself. This solution provides a more consistent transportation system, significantly lowers the operating costs of current transportation systems, and has a smaller environmental impact.

  • Successfully developed and tested on the Georgia Tech campus.
  • Can be implemented with a budget of max $100 million in a max of 10 years.
  • Will help your campus reach its sustainability goals faster than ever. Will inspire your corresponding city to adopt our novel idea and help reduce the global net emissions caused by transportation.

            I have allergies, can I take Humalev? 
            
    
              
    
                Yes! Anyone can take Humalev. This isn't the 21st century anymore...Our super smart scientists have bioengineered the perfect pill that no one is allergic to.

            I sometimes spin uncontrollably on the Hover Rail. How do I stop? My head hurts. 
            
    
              
    
                Dang. First of all, sorry man. It'll be okay. Just make sure to pay close attention to the training next time. All you have to do is put your hand up in a V position to trigger your magnetic brakes. The higher your hands, the stricter of a brake.
